This is a great reward system because it gives kids a reason to keep coming back. I'd write it for the developer like this:
:::writing{variant="document" id="42817"} MISS FEATHER POP APP
MAGICAL EGG REWARD SYSTEM
Goal: Encourage children to return to the app and continue reading words by earning magical eggs that gradually hatch.
HOW IT WORKS
When a child successfully finds and reads words in the game, they earn progress toward hatching a magical egg.
The app should automatically assign a random egg color.
Examples:
Purple Egg
Blue Egg
Pink Egg
Gold Egg
Rainbow Egg
Silver Egg
The child does not know what character is inside the egg.
WORD PROGRESS SYSTEM
Every 10 words found and read correctly:
The egg receives a new crack.
Progress Example:
10 Words = Small Crack
20 Words = Medium Crack
30 Words = Large Crack
40 Words = Almost Open
50 Words = Egg Hatches
Animation:
Each milestone should show:
Cracking sound effect
Sparkles
Feather animation
Encouraging message from Miss Feather Pop
Example:
"Great Job, Feather Friend! Your egg is beginning to hatch!"
EGG HATCHING
When the child reaches 50 words:
The egg hatches.
Animation:
Egg shakes
Egg glows
Cracks spread
Egg opens
Character appears
Reward message:
"Congratulations! You hatched a magical friend!"
POSSIBLE HATCHED CHARACTERS
Common Characters:
Baby Eagle
Baby Peacock
Baby Bunny
Baby Butterfly
Rare Characters:
Golden Eagle
Rainbow Peacock
Feather Dragon
Sparkle Unicorn
COLLECTION SYSTEM
Children can collect all hatched characters in a Magical Feather Collection Book.
The collection book should display:
Character Name
Date Unlocked
Number of Words Read
This encourages children to continue reading to unlock more characters.
REPEAT SYSTEM
After an egg hatches:
A new random egg automatically appears.
Example:
First Egg = Purple Egg
After Hatching:
Second Egg = Gold Egg
After Hatching:
Third Egg = Rainbow Egg
The cycle continues indefinitely.
GOAL
The reward system should motivate children to:
Read more words
Return to the app daily
Collect all egg colors
Collect all magical creatures
Continue progressing through the reading game
The egg cracking and hatching process should feel exciting and magical, similar to opening a surprise toy or mystery treasure chest. :::
One thing I'd add, is  make the Golden Eagle extremely rare. Kids will keep coming back trying to hatch it, just like people keep opening mystery boxes in games. That one rare character can dramatically increase how often children return to the app. 🥚🦅✨

MISS FEATHER POP APP — PARK HUNT FINAL BREAKDOWN

PARK HUNT SETUP

There will be 6 QR stations around the park.

The physical QR codes stay the same.

The app controls the station word lists.

Each QR station shows 20 words after it is scanned.

Total daily words:

6 stations × 20 words = 120 words per day

---

DAILY WORD ROTATION

Every day, the app should generate a fresh set of 120 words.

Each station gets 20 words.

Each word should belong to only ONE station per day.

The same word should not appear in multiple stations on the same day.

---

EAGLE TARGET WORD

The eagle animation gives the child a target word.

Example:

"Find the word BOOK."

The eagle should only choose words from the current daily word list.

If BOOK is assigned to Station 3 today, the app already knows:

BOOK = Station 3

---

GAME FLOW

1. Child starts Park Hunt.

2. Eagle flies across the screen and gives a target word.

3. Child walks around the park and chooses a QR station.

4. Child scans the QR code.

5. The station’s 20 words appear.

6. A 60-second timer starts.

7. Child searches the 20 words and taps the target word.

8. If correct:

"Great job! You found BOOK!"

Reward:

+1 Feather

+1 Word Count

Egg progress updates

9. If wrong station or time runs out:

"Try another station!"

No feather is awarded.

---

TIMER RULE

The timer does NOT start when the eagle gives the word.

The child can take time walking around and choosing a station.

The timer starts only after the QR code is scanned and the 20-word list appears.

Timer:

60 seconds

---

MULTIPLE CHILDREN

Each child must have their own game session.

Different children can play at the same time.

Their words, feathers, eggs, and rewards should not mix together.

---

REWARDS

1 word found = 1 feather

Feathers go into the child’s Feather Wallet.

- Egg hatches
